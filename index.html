About Us

Greenacre Eagles Football Club is a proud member of the Bankstown District Amateur Football Association, representing the suburb of Greenacre in western Sydney. Over the years, the Eagles have built a strong reputation as a competitive and community‑minded club, bringing together players of all ages and backgrounds to enjoy the game and represent our green, black and white colours with pride.

In 2024, the club won the District League premiership and earned promotion into the Bankstown Premier League, where we now compete among the area’s top teams. From juniors through to senior squads, Greenacre Eagles FC is committed to fostering local talent, teamwork and a welcoming environment for everyone who loves football.
